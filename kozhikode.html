Explore Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a coastal city with a rich history and diverse culture. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Kozhikode Beach and experience the warm hospitality of this enchanting destination.

Take a stroll along the historic Kappad Beach, where Vasco da Gama first set foot in India. Explore the ancient temples and vibrant markets, offering a glimpse into the traditions of the region.
